Recipe Steps for ca 50 cookies

Step 1: Ingredients

Gather all the Ingredients you will need. You need: 400 grams flour, 200 grams butter, 100 grams powdered sugar, 1 egg, 1 pinch of salt

Step 2: Measure Flour

Carefully measure the flour to ensure accuracy.

Step 3: Powder Sugar Size

If you add the powdered sugar, I would recommend sieving it to make sure it doesn't remain in too large chunks

Step 4: Preview

Here you can see how it should look like before blending.

Step 5: Tools for Mixing

Prepare tools like mixers or bowls.

Step 6: Knead Dough

After blending it enough, knead the dough until it becomes smooth.

Step 7: Chill Dough

Place the dough in the fridge to cool. I would recommend leaving it there for about 30 minutes.

Step 8: Roll Out Dough

Roll out the dough with your rolling pin to your desired thickness.

Step 9: Cut Cookies

Now you can use the molds to press out your cookies

Step 10: Place on Tray

Arrange the cookies on the baking tray.

Step 11: Preheat Oven

Preheat the oven to 200 degrees top bottom.

Step 12: Bake Cookies

Bake the cookies until golden brown and then your finished. Enjoy!
